--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About Meng Wu (邬萌) joined Ant Financial(Shanghai) in July 2019, to work on blockchain safety and security. He got his PhD at Virginia Tech, advised by Prof. [Chao Wang](https://sites.usc.edu/chaowang/). He received a B.S. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China. His research is to utilize formal methods and program analysis techniques to improve software safety and security.
